About Us

Mom is a great cook. She has all kinds of recipes written on small pieces of paper, typed on mimeo, stuck between pages of other recipes in cookbooks. I realized that technology had finally caught enough that instead of having all of those wonderful examples of food sitting around in a filing system that only she seemed to know maybe I could put together a little corner of the internets that would be a place to store those wonderful Christmas candies and the goopy cinnamon rolls that only she seems to be able to make. So here it is, my version of small pieces of paper, mimeo and sticky 3x5 cards.
